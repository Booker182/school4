Related courses:

Our school offers up to nine courses. It includes compulsory courses for students before college. There are mathematics, English, chemistry, geography, physics, history, politics, biology, information technology. We have professional teachers to teach, and each course has a corresponding professional teacher. There are also teachers in different grades. The teaching contents and teaching methods are different. Each teacher has mastered all the contents they teach, which is an absolute guarantee for the teaching quality of our school.

1 month course plan for each course:

The math class will study limits, functions and sequences within a month. English class will learn words, sentence patterns and grammar within one month. The chemistry class will study the understanding of elements, organic chemistry and the characteristics of elements within one month. The geography class will learn the relevant geographical knowledge of China, world geography and some special geographical knowledge within one month. Physics class will study momentum, potential energy and force within one month. The history class will learn about China's history, world history and some important historical events within one month. The politics class will study Chinese politics, world politics and some important political events within one month. The biology class will study the impact of the environment on organisms, ecosystems and cells within one month. The information technology class will learn HTML, CSS and Java within one month.
